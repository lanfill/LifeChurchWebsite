How do I register?

Register online at www.lifechurchwi.com for only $10 from now until Dec 3rd. On Dec 3rd registration online will be $15. At the door price is $15

 

Do I have to register?

Registration helps us keep appropriate child/worker ratios as well as help plan facility use and snacks. Thanks for your consideration.

 

What ages is Parent Night Out for?

Birth through 5th Grade

 

Will you feed my child?

Popcorn and a drink will be provided during the movie

 

When can I drop my child off?

Doors open at 6:00pm

 

What time do I pick my child up?

Please be back to pick up your child no later than 9pm.

 

How do I pay?

Pay by Debit or Credit Card online! Cash at the door please.

 

What will my child do while I am gone?

We will have some fun activities set up as well as watching Toy Story 3! Video games and computers will be available as well.

 

What about an Emergency?

When you drop your child off, we will ask for a cell phone number incase of an emergency.

 

Is this just for LifeChurch?

No! If you have friends and neighbors that would like to utilize Parent Night Out, please send them to www.lifechurchwi.com to register.
